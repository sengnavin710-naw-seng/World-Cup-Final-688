# Context

## Terms

**Participant**
A person using the World Cup selection site.

**National Team**
One of the 48 World Cup teams available for selection.

**Team Selection**
The single team currently chosen by a Participant.

**Selection Rule**
Each Participant has exactly one Team Selection at a time.

**Returning Participant**
A Participant who opens the site again on the same device and is recognized without repeating the selection step.

**Device Identity**
A browser-bound identifier used to recognize the same Participant on the same device.

**Device Reset**
An explicit action that clears the current Device Identity and local participant memory so a new Participant can start again on the same device.

**Selection Release**
The act of returning an owned National Team to the available state when the owning Participant intentionally resets their device identity.

**Display Name**
A short participant-provided name used to identify a Participant in shared tournament records.

**Selection Change**
An action where a Participant replaces their current Team Selection with another National Team after explicit confirmation.

**Home View**
The main view shown after selection. It presents tournament information across all teams while emphasizing the Participant's current Team Selection.

**Team Selection Grid**
A visual selection surface that presents all National Teams as cards with flag and team name.

**Team Search**
A filter input used to narrow the Team Selection Grid by team name and supported aliases.

**Selection Confirmation**
The act of finalizing the currently highlighted National Team and entering the Home View.

**Brand Header**
The left side of the top navigation that displays the product logo and brand name.

**Home Tabs**
The set of primary sections inside the Home View: Knockout, Fixtures, Table, and News.

**Selected Team Summary**
A persistent summary area in the Home View that displays the Participant's current National Team and provides access to Selection Change.

**Notification Panel**
A lightweight panel opened from the bell icon that shows placeholder updates relevant to the tournament.

**Mock Tournament Data**
Non-production tournament content used to drive the first version of the experience before live data integration.

**Selection Record**
A stored record that links a Participant identity and Display Name to a Team Selection for shared reporting.

**Team Availability**
The current state of a National Team as either available for selection or already owned by a Participant.

**Selection Conflict**
An attempted Selection Confirmation that fails because another Participant secured the same National Team first.

**Company Picks Table**
A shared view that summarizes which Participants currently own which National Teams.

**Team Spotlight News**
News content that highlights the Participant's current National Team within the broader tournament news feed.
